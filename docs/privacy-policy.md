# WhaTo — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 16, 2026

WhaTo is a free app that helps groups decide what to eat or watch. We built it because we needed it, and we respect your privacy. Here's what happens with your data — spoiler: not much.

## What We Collect

### On Your Device Only (never sent to us)
- **Display name** — The name you enter for group sessions. Stored locally on your device so you don't have to re-enter it.
- **Device ID** — A randomly generated identifier (UUID) stored on your device. Used to identify you within a group session. It is not tied to your real identity.

### Shared During Group Sessions
- **Session data** — When you join a group session, your display name and swipe choices are shared with other participants via Firebase Realtime Database. This data is temporary and automatically expires after 24 hours.
- **Location (food sessions only)** — If you choose the food topic, the app requests your location to find nearby restaurants. Your location is sent directly to Yelp and Google Places to fetch results. We do not store your location on our servers.

### What We Do NOT Collect
- No email addresses
- No phone numbers
- No real names (beyond the display name you choose)
- No advertising identifiers
- No analytics or usage tracking
- No crash reports sent to us
- No cookies or web tracking

## Third-Party Services

WhaTo uses these services to function. Each has their own privacy policy:

- **Firebase Realtime Database** ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)) — Stores temporary group session data. Sessions auto-delete after 24 hours.
- **Yelp Fusion API** ([Yelp Privacy Policy](https://www.yelp.com/tos/privacy_policy)) — Provides restaurant data. Your approximate location is sent to Yelp when searching for food.
- **The Movie Database (TMDB) API** ([TMDB Privacy Policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy)) — Provides movie and TV show data. No personal data is sent to TMDB.
- **Google Places API** ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)) — Provides supplementary restaurant photos. Your approximate location may be sent to Google when searching for food.

## SMS Invitations

WhaTo allows you to invite friends to group sessions via SMS. This opens your device's native messaging app — we never access your contacts or send messages on your behalf.

## Data Retention

- **Local data** (display name, device ID) stays on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
- **Session data** in Firebase automatically expires and is deleted after 24 hours.
- We have no mechanism to retain data beyond these periods because we don't have user accounts or persistent storage.

## Children's Privacy

WhaTo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app collects virtually no personal information from anyone, there is minimal risk — but if you believe a child has somehow provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data or maintain user accounts, there is nothing to delete or export. Your locally stored data (display name, device ID) can be removed by uninstalling the app. Session data expires automatically within 24 hours.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we'll post the new version here with an updated effective date. We won't reduce your privacy protections without notice.
